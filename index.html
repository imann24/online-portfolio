About

Senior engineer with a decade of shipping —
now squarely focused on AI.

I'm a Senior Software Engineer II at Committee for Children, with prior experience at Amazon, Qualtrics, true[X], and Infillion. These days I spend most of my time in the AI stack — building agents, evals, prompt pipelines, and the developer tooling that turns LLMs from impressive demos into dependable products.

Before that, I co-founded a game studio at Hampshire College and shipped a portfolio of games and interactive experiences in Unity. That craft — designing systems people actually want to come back to — informs everything I build today.

I care about inclusive teams, thoughtful architecture, and software that earns its place in someone's life.
